Design

We've gone for a student-themed website, with the main pages based on a wooden desktop.

E-mail inbox and folders are displayed on a paper file, the e-mails themselves are listed on Post-It notes, and the e-mail content appears as if written on lined paper.

It's a colourful and friendly theme, but intuitive. Everything is neatly laid out and easy to identify.
